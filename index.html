About this Museum

This digital museum explores how Filipino popular culture has evolved from 2000 to 2030 through three key themes: affect & feelings, performance, and globalization. It traces how teleseryes, OPM music, live shows, online platforms, and global media shaped the way Filipinos express emotion, build community, and participate in the world.

By presenting the past, present, and future of pop culture, this museum preserves cultural memory while showing how each generation transforms shared experiences into new forms of creativity and identity.
